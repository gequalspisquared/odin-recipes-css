Proatmeal

Description

Yummy yummy protein oatmeal (proatmeal) for packing on HUGE amounts of muscle!

Ingredients

This recipe is very simple and only needs a few ingredients.

  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 scoops protein powder
  • 1/2 cup whole milk

Steps

Making proatmeal is easy and can be done in 5-minutes the morning of.

  1. Combine the oats, peanut butter, and protein powder in a bowl
  2. Warm the milk in a pot on medium heat, stirring occasionally under the milk begins to evaporate
  3. Pour the milk in the bowl and mix with a spoon
