Ice Cream

Description

I'll be honest, I have no idea how to make ice cream. But I do know how to buy it.

Ingredients

I guess these aren't ingredients in the usual sense, but whatever!

  • A mode of transportation
  • A place that sells ice cream
  • Money

Steps

I mean its pretty obvious from here, but I'll spell it out.

  1. Use the mode of transportation to go to the place that sells ice cream
  2. Buy the ice cream
  3. Eat the ice cream

That is how one consumes ice cream.
